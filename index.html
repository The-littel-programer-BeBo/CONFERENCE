International Conference on New Horizons in Basic and Applied Science of 2023

The 6th. International Conference on New Horizons in Basic and Applied Science (ICNHBAS 2023) aims to review the priorities of scientific research within the framework of the Faculty of Science interest in supporting the Sustainable Development Strategy, Egypt vision 2030 (SDS) , and contribute to achieving strategy’s objectives in different areas of development in general and in innovation, knowledge and scientific research in particular.

The focus of innovation, knowledge and scientific research is one of the axes of the twelve strategic of Egypt vision 2030 (SDS). The strategic vision for knowledge, innovation, and scientific research focuses on accomplishing three main objectives including production of knowledge, activation of an innovation system, and linking the knowledge and the innovation outputs with priorities.

These objectives will be covered in ICNHBAS 2023 through the papers presented in the different sessions (Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Biology, and Geology). Egypt vision 2030 (SDS) focus also on Environment (one of three dimensions of the sustainable development strategy 2030) which is integrated in all economic sectors to preserve natural resources and support their efficient use and investment, while ensuring next generations’ rights. Our ICNHBAS 2023 have a special session entitled (Environmental and Agricultural Sciences) which deals with all the environmental aspects especially those taken by Egypt 2030 as indicators to ensure a clean, safe and healthy environment. These indicators included water consumption, renewable freshwater resources, pollution reduction and so on.

ICNHBAS 2023 will host numerous keynote speakers from distinguished scientists from all over the world, to interact with the experts in their fields and to foster interdisciplinary collaborations required to meet the challenges of modern science, technology and society. ICNHBAS 2023 promotes for the participants to exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish business or research relations and to find global partners for future collaboration.

ICNHBAS 2023 is one of the leading international conferences for presenting novel and fundamental researches in the fields of basic and applied sciences. It serves to foster communication among researchers and practitioners working in a wide variety of scientific areas.

On behalf of the Organizing Committee of the 6th. International Conference on New Horizons in Basic and Applied Science (ICNHBAS 2023), we are pleased to invite academic scientists, scholars, engineers and students from the universities, technologists, entrepreneurs and policy makers all around the World to attend the 6th. ICNHBAS 2023 activities which will be held during 22-25 September, 2023, Hurghada, Egypt

Looking forwards to meet you at ICNHBAS 2023 in Hurghada, Egypt.
